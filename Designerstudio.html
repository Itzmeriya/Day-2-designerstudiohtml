Welcome to Designer's Studio


Serving the central Pennsylvania region since 1987!

Designer’s Studio was built with our customers and clients in mind. At over 15,000 square feet, our showroom has become the leading destination for interior design and home décor in central Pennsylvania. Our one-stop shopping experience is for homeowners and decorators alike featuring extensive collections of furnishings, fabrics, window treatments, wall coverings, floor coverings, artwork, lighting, tabletop, and much more, all under one roof.


Our design center offers visitors a chance to physically see things in person before making decisions about a project, as well as consider unlimited options for customization. We encourage you to ask questions, bring your design ideas, fabric swatches, colors, and magazine clippings. We're here to help design a home, a room, or unique area within that you'll be proud to call your own.


“Despite extensive online resources, our customers still want to see and touch what they’re buying, especially with textiles but also with furniture in terms of scale. You can look at a hundred fabrics online and not know anything about them. If you’re lucky enough to be in central Pennsylvania, stop in and see us. We make it easy to understand all the possibilities.” ~ Geri Jordan, Lead Designer
